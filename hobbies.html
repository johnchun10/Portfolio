My Hobbies

I try my best to enjoy life so I push myself to try new things everyday.
Here are a few of my everyday hobbies that I've adopted over the years.

ROWING

I've recently started rowing due to me constantly injuring myself during soccer. Rowing involves much more linear movement which is perfect for my body. I enjoy rowing because it is a great source of exercise and it involves much precision in your form and stroke.

READING

Ever since I was little, I've enjoyed reading. Reading would take me to many different worlds where my imagination would be able to run wild. I still read today, albeit less than before. I picture reading as almost a form of meditation for me.

GAMING

Out of all of my hobbies, I would say this is my guilty pleasure. I believe I spoil myself too much in gaming because it is so immersive for me. A lot of my friends spend their time online playing games along with me. While I do believe gaming in general is not unhealty for you, I do believe in self-limitation which is why I need to learn this technique as quickly as possible!

CELLO

I used to play the piano along with the cello but I moved full time over to cello. I do have to say the cello is harder than some other instruments but I believe it is worth it to learn it beacuse of the outcome of the beautiful music you can make.
